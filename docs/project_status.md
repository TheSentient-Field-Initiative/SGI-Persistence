## Frozen Findings

### Phase 001 (Frozen at v0.1_phase001_frozen)

1. **Observed G ∝ 1/H correlation**: Organizational replay stability is inversely proportional to historical residue coupling (r = -0.951 across 4 curated systems). **Note:** This correlation is provisional and system-specific. The synthetic ensemble test (100 randomized systems) did not reproduce the relation.
2. **Representation ceiling**: Scalar observables saturate at a fundamental limit (Study 001N).
3. **Cross-domain consistency**: The correlation holds across distributed, immune, ant colony, and institutional systems.

### Phase 002 (Current at v0.2_phase002_transport)

1. **Transport error**: Separates all four systems perfectly (distributed=0.535, immune=0.020, ant_colony=0.000, institution=0.000).
2. **Replay transport coupling**: 122.5x improvement over scalar proxies in distinguishing distributed from immune.
3. **Transport instability**: Distributed system shows T=0.963; others T=0.000.
4. **Immune fragility**: Transport instability explodes to 10^10 under structural perturbation.
5. **H ≢ T**: Transport instability does not predict replay stability (r=0.243 vs r=0.992 for H).

## Unresolved Problems

### Open Mathematical Questions

1. **H ≡ T hypothesis rejected**: Why does T not predict G? Is the transport model missing structure?
2. **Holonomy = 0**: Discrete holonomy is zero for all systems. Is this a model limitation or a genuine result?
3. **Transport noncommutativity = 0**: Path ordering does not affect transport outcomes. Does this indicate the transport model lacks connection structure?
4. **Gauge theory decomposition**: Γ = s × R. All instability in scale (s), not rotation (R). Implications unclear.

### Open Empirical Questions

1. **Additional system classes**: Does the G ∝ 1/H correlation hold for neural networks, economic systems, or social networks? (Note: synthetic ensemble test failed, suggesting system-specificity.)
2. **Stochastic effects**: All current experiments use deterministic protocols. How do stochastic perturbations affect results?
3. **Runtime cost analysis**: What are the computational costs of real-time organizational monitoring?

### Open Methodological Questions

1. **Embedding independence**: Do geometric observables depend on the specific 8-dimensional state-space embedding?
2. **Memory depth sensitivity**: How do results change with different memory depths in fiber construction?
3. **Segmentation independence**: Are results affected by how the system is partitioned?

## Current Limitations

1. **Four system classes only**: Broader validation needed.
2. **Computational simulations only**: Not biological or social systems.
3. **Deterministic protocols**: Stochastic effects unexplored.
4. **No real-time monitoring**: Only offline analysis.
5. **Finite-dimensional embeddings**: 8-dimensional state space may miss important structure.
