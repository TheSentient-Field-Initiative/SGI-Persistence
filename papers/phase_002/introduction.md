Phase 001 established the empirical law G ∝ 1/H across four system classes. ## Geometric Approach

We model organizational state space as a fiber bundle, where:
- **Base space (M)**: The manifold of observable organizational states
- **Fiber (F)**: The historical residue attached to each state
- **Bundle (B = M × F)**: The combined structure

This framework enables:
1. **Transport error**: Measuring fiber consistency along trajectories
2. **Replay transport coupling**: Quantifying historical entanglement
3. **Transport instability**: Assessing path dependence under perturbation
